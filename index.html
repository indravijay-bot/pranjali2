Our Love Story

1 Nov 2021

We’ve Met: A Tale of Love Unveiled

"In the symphony of our arranged union, each note resonates with the melody of love, transforming the script of arrangement into a poetic dance of hearts."
In the serendipitous dance of destiny, Anjali and I found each other through the echoes of phone calls and the intimacy of video screens. Our connection, born in pixels, became a symphony of shared dreams and laughter. Our families, guided by tradition, sealed our commitment with virtual rings, marking the beginning of a love story that transcended digital realms. Finally, we met at a picturesque resort, where the warmth of our first embrace melted away the distance that once separated us. As the sun dipped below the horizon, our hearts beat in synchrony, and the resort became a canvas for the tangible celebration of our love.

8 Nov 2021

First Date: Life's First Satisfactory Dance

On the eighth of November, Anjali and I shared our first date at a romantic restaurant. The ambiance was enchanting, with soft lights casting a warm glow. As we indulged in a feast for the senses, laughter and shared emotions filled the air. The evening unfolded like a love poem, each moment etching itself into our hearts. Underneath the November sky, amidst the aroma of delectable cuisine, our first date became a chapter in the story of us—a promise of more shared moments and the beginning of a beautiful journey.

15 Nov 2021

Physical Engagement & Family Function

November 15th marked more than just a date; it was a symphony of love and family. Surrounded by dear ones, we celebrated our physical engagement, a joyous union of hearts and families. In the midst of laughter and festivity, Anjali and I exchanged rings, each symbolizing a promise of forever. The room shimmered with the warmth of love, and the air was fragrant with the shared joy of our loved ones. Amidst the celebrations, our quiet meet-up with loved ones added layers to the evening—each smile, hug, and shared glance narrating tales of love and support. As the stars witnessed our joy, November 15th became a chapter in the love story we continue to write—a day when the brilliance of our engagement echoed in the laughter, stories, and shared moments with those who make our journey extraordinary.

17 Dec 2022

Reunion: A Tale of Patience and Love

Visa struggles conquered, Anjali's arrival at Melbourne Airport marked our sweet reunion. In our reunion, eyes spoke volumes—joy eclipsing the shadows of separation. The journey from the airport to our shared haven unfolded with palpable anticipation, relief, and profound gratitude. My room, meticulously adorned, became a sanctuary where shared dreams and renewed promises pulsated with love. Every detail, from soft lights to arranged flowers, whispered the resilience that fueled our journey. Our story, momentarily halted by bureaucratic challenges, resumed its course—a testament to patience, endurance, and the unwavering strength of love across miles.
